# Nutrient-Rich Yoruba Dishes (Nigeria)

This repository documents **traditional Yoruba dishes** with a focus on **nutrient density, whole foods, and cultural accuracy**.  
The structure follows **GitHub Flavored Markdown (GFM)** for clarity, collaboration, and long-term maintainability.

---

## Ẹ̀fọ́ Ríró (Leafy Green Stew)

### Primary Ingredients
- Spinach or efo tete
- Palm oil
- Tomatoes and peppers
- Onions
- Locust beans (iru)
- Fish or assorted meats

### Key Nutrients
- Iron
- Vitamin A (beta-carotene)
- Vitamin K
- Protein
- Healthy fats

### Notes
A nutritionally complete dish when prepared with ample greens and moderate palm oil.

---

## Ewedu Soup (Jute Leaf Soup)

### Primary Ingredients
- Jute leaves
- Locust beans
- Salt and seasoning

### Key Nutrients
- Dietary fiber
- Vitamin C
- Calcium
- Antioxidants

### Notes
Commonly paired with amala; supports digestion due to its mucilaginous texture.

---

## Amala (Yam Flour Swallow)

### Primary Ingredients
- Elubo (yam flour)

### Key Nutrients
- Complex carbohydrates
- Potassium
- Vitamin B6
- Dietary fiber

### Notes
Energy-dense staple best consumed with nutrient-rich soups.

---

## Gbegiri (Bean Soup)

### Primary Ingredients
- Brown beans (peeled)
- Palm oil
- Peppers
- Onions

### Key Nutrients
- Plant-based protein
- Fiber
- Folate
- Magnesium
- Iron

### Notes
Complements amala and ewedu to create a balanced meal.

---

## Ọbẹ̀ Ata Dindin (Fried Pepper Stew)

### Primary Ingredients
- Peppers
- Tomatoes
- Onions
- Palm oil or vegetable oil
- Fish or meat

### Key Nutrients
- Vitamin C
- Lycopene
- Healthy fats

### Notes
Cooking enhances lycopene bioavailability from tomatoes.

---

## Ikokore (Ijebu Water Yam Pottage)

### Primary Ingredients
- Water yam
- Palm oil
- Peppers
- Fish
- Crayfish
- Leafy greens

### Key Nutrients
- Complex carbohydrates
- Fiber
- Potassium
- Protein
- Micronutrients from greens

### Notes
A whole-food-centered Yoruba dish with minimal refinement.

---

## Asaro (Yam Porridge)

### Primary Ingredients
- Yam
- Palm oil
- Peppers
- Onions
- Fish
- Leafy vegetables

### Key Nutrients
- Complex carbohydrates
- Vitamin B6
- Potassium
- Protein
- Healthy fats

### Notes
More balanced than boiled yam due to added fats and vegetables.

---

## Ewa Agoyin (Mashed Beans with Pepper Sauce)

### Primary Ingredients
- Brown beans
- Onions
- Dried peppers
- Palm oil

### Key Nutrients
- High fiber
- Plant protein
- Iron
- Slow-digesting carbohydrates

### Notes
Supports satiety and blood sugar stability when oil is used moderately.
